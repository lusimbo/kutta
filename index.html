About KUTTA

KUTTA is a student-led organization at Kenyatta University dedicated to advancing telecommunications and technology. Founded by Mr. Jack Bruno Siso, who also serves as the chairman, KUTTA brings together students passionate about networking, programming, artificial intelligence, and cutting-edge innovations in the tech industry.

KUTTA: Innovating Together, Leading the Future of Technology.

A vibrant team of KUTTA members engaged in discussion and collaboration, reflecting the spirit of teamwork and innovation that defines the organization. Driven by a shared passion for telecommunications and technology, they actively participate in networking, programming, artificial intelligence, and cutting-edge research. Their diverse skills and backgrounds contribute to a dynamic environment where ideas are exchanged, challenges are tackled, and groundbreaking solutions are developed. Through mentorship, hands-on projects, and industry engagements, KUTTA empowers its members to grow professionally while shaping the future of technology.
