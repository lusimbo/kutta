Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Membership & Registration

  • How to join KUTTA?
    You can join KUTTA by visiting our website and filling out the membership form.
  • How to renew my membership?
    Membership renewal can be done through your profile settings on our website.
  • How to cancel my membership?
    To cancel, go to account settings and click on 'Cancel Membership'.

Events & Webinars

  • How to register for an event?
    You can register for an event through the Events section on our website.
  • How to attend a webinar?
    After registering, you will receive a link via email to join the webinar.
  • How to get event materials?
    Event materials are available for download on the event page after the webinar.
