Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Kenyatta University Telecommunications & Technology Association (KUTTA), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, student ID, and other details you provide.

Non-Personal Data: We may collect non-personal data such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.

Cookies & Tracking Data: We use cookies and analytics tools to track user behavior on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve KUTTA services.
  • Manage memberships, events, and communications.
  • Analyze website usage for improvements.
  • Send newsletters, updates, or event notifications (with opt-out options).

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

KUTTA does NOT sell or rent user data. Information may be shared with:

  • University administration for membership verification.
  • Event organizers for participation management.
  • Third-party services for analytics and security.
  • Legal compliance: Data may be shared if required by law.

5. Data Storage & Security

We store user data securely and implement measures to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, or cyber threats. Sensitive data is protected using encryption standards and password protection.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, analytics, and tracking tools to enhance user experience. Users can disable cookies in their browser settings. We use third-party tools like Google Analytics and social media plugins.

7. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website may contain links to external resources. KUTTA is not responsible for third-party privacy policies.

8. User Rights & Data Control

Users have the right to access, edit, or delete their personal data. They can also opt-out of communications. For privacy-related requests, please contact us.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for minors. For users under 18, guardian or parental consent is required.

10. Policy Updates & Changes

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes. Users will be informed of significant updates.
